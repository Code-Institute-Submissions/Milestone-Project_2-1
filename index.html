About

01

Why One More Team

Following your team doesn't quite satisfy your football passion?
Maybe moved to a new country, and need inspiration for a team more local to support?
Whatever the reason, One More Team is here to help with that very inspiration - helping you to find one more team to support

02

How It Works

Version 1.0 provides you a team recommendation based on current league position. Leagues available to choose from include:

  • English Premier League
  • French Ligue 1
  • Italian Serie A
  • Spanish La Liga

All you need to do is:

  1. Select the league(s)
  2. Select a team
  3. Team recommendation is generated, along with stats about each team

03

Future Features

Here's a taste of what is to come in future releases:

  • More leagues to choose from
  • Filter which stats you want your team recommendation to be based on
  • Subscribe feature, updating you on your new-found team
  • Filter for time range
  • Team recommendation feature also generates information that can redirect users to team websites/social media
